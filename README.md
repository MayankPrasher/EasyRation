**EasyRation**, tackles the **challenges of the current ration distribution system**.
We are introducing atoken and **appointment system to eliminate long queues**, allowing beneficiaries to **schedule their visit**. 
A user-friendly interface lets**people find the nearest store** and let know about **stock availability in advance to ration shopkeeper**. 
Behind the scenes, our centralized database provides **real-time inventory management** to **prevent stockouts** and **ensure transparency**.
Most importantly, **EasyRation connects all ration stores into a single nationwide network**, giving people the **freedom to collect their rations from any authorized shop across India**.
